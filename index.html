Jonah Lipson is a sports broadcast journalist and production assistant with experience across live television, radio, and digital media. He works with Syracuse Athletics Broadcast Production, contributing to ACC Network and FloSports broadcasts in roles ranging from producer and graphics operator to camera operator and replay. Jonah also covers Syracuse athletics for Z89 Sports, where he produces radio broadcasts, reports on games, and creates on-air and social content. Previously, he served as a play-by-play announcer and color commentator for the San Francisco Seagulls Baseball organization and worked in studio production at CitrusTV Sports.
